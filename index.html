About

I am a Data Analyst with hands-on experience in SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Python, focused on transforming raw data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making. I have worked on real-world datasets across domains such as healthcare, finance, sales, and automation, building KPI-driven dashboards, analytical reports, and predictive models.

My work includes developing interactive Power BI dashboards, performing data cleaning and exploratory data analysis, automating reports using Python, and building machine learning models for health risk prediction and process optimization. Alongside analytics, I am actively building expertise in Data Science and AI/ML, with hands-on projects involving Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Streamlit, and Generative AI. I enjoy solving business problems through data, continuously learning new technologies, and converting analytical insights into practical, impactful solutions.
